## What Validation-OS is for

Make a product team better at deciding — experiment-driven, by consensus,
on the evidence. It's a decision-making tool, not a tracker.

## Design principles

Everything you build or change is judged against these. They're the tie-break
when a call isn't obvious — no ranking among them; hold all three at once.

- **Clarity** — less noisy, more intuitive. Hide the machinery; explain
  visually, not in prose. If the user has to think about the mechanism, the
  design failed.
- **Shared understanding** — decisions and their reasoning are visible to the
  whole team, never smuggled in. One vocabulary, one register.
- **Speed** — the above, as fast as it can honestly go.

**Progress must be felt — in the dashboard.** Every landed experiment visibly
moves Confidence and Risk, and the register reorders in front of you. If a
week's work didn't move the surface, the dashboard failed to show it.
