# Evidence source — Gmail (email)

Active when `gmail` ∈ the config's `evidence_sources`. Requires the Gmail
MCP server connected in the harness.

**What it holds:** direct written statements from customers, partners, and
prospects — often the strongest *stated* evidence you have, and sometimes
genuine commitment (a signed-off intent, a confirmed order) in writing.

**How to search:** search threads by the assumption's subject terms and by
the names/domains of the people the Lens points at; read full threads —
context changes the reading of a single message.

**Qualifies as evidence when:** the counterparty (matching the Lens) states
a fact, describes past behaviour (`Talk`), or commits to something
costly in writing (`Commitment` — e.g. an LOI attached, an explicit
purchase confirmation). An enthusiastic "sounds great!" is
`Talk` at best.

**Caveats:**
- Email is the natural join key across sources — the same person's email
  address links their calls (Fireflies), CRM record (Attio), and threads.
- Date = when the email was sent; source link = a durable thread reference.
