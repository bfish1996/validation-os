## EXP-001: Reconciliation-pain interviews (example)
- **Assumption**: ASM-001
- **Type**: Anecdotal
- **Source quality**: Medium
- **Feasibility**: High
- **We're right if**: ≥6 of 10 SMB owners describe, unprompted, a recurring
  manual reconciliation routine when asked about last month's bookkeeping.
- **Result**: Running
- **Strength**: 0           <!-- derived: 0 until a conclusive Result -->
- **Owner**: you

### Protocol
Interview template (`skills/_shared/experiment-guardrails.md §3`): recruit 10
SMB owners; ask about the last time they closed their books, not whether they
"would" use anything.

### We're wrong if
≤2 of 10 mention manual reconciliation at all, or most describe it as a solved
non-issue.
